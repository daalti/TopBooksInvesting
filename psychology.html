Thinking, Fast and Slow

Buy on Amazon

I've learned so much about how my mind functions because to this book. The two systems of the human spirit are described in Daniel Kahneman's book Thinking Fast and Slow. System 1 is the more impulsive and automatic thinking that we have. System 2 on the other hand is the conscious and aware mind that enables us to reason through specific issues in order to arrive at logical judgments. This book is relevant to personal finance because many investors make impulsive, irrational decisions that cause system 1 to interfere. Investors must apply both systems without letting one take precedence over the other. While making difficult investment decisions, this book will assist you in achieving both thinking systems.
