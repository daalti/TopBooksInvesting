Trade Like Warren Buffett

Buy on Amazon

While Warren Buffett is considered the "world's greatest value investor," there's another side to Buffett that is rarely talked about. Although Buffett has gained recognition for his value investing approach to the markets, the fact is that nobody-over the past fifty years-has traded and invested with a more diverse group of strategies than Buffett.

Trade Like Warren Buffett challenges the current coverage of this great investor by including details of all of Buffett's investing and trading methods, including mean reversion, commodities, bonds, arbitrage, market timing, funds, as well as Graham-Dodd. To augment the discussion of each strategy, Trade Like Warren Buffett also includes interviews with leading financial professionals, who reveal in detail how they've successfully used the same techniques.

There is no one way to sum up Warren Buffett's investment style. But if you're interested in boosting the performance of your portfolio, Trade Like Warren Buffett can show you how.

The Hedge Fund Edge

Buy on Amazon

Any investor or trader who wants to consistently benefit from the markets without taking significant risks must read The Hedge Fund Edge. Hedge fund manager and well-known trade speaker Mark Boucher offers readers a sound strategy for earning market-beating, long-term returns with risk that is significantly lower than the long-term risk of U.S. and international equities.

Following a brief examination of the drawbacks of conventional stock and bond investment, Boucher goes on to explain how to identify the most secure and lucrative times to buy equities worldwide. He provides a conceptual explanation of this tactic in addition to using an objective model that has been successfully applied to money management since the 1950s. He demonstrates how to distribute money among international stocks at any given time while adhering to profitable, dependable, and safe patterns. The Austrian Liquidity Cycle, a novel synthesis of Austrian Economics, Economic Alchemy, and Liquidity Cycle Theory, is also extensively covered in the book. Boucher demonstrates how to apply this theory to comprehend the main market movements and choose the most lucrative market to invest in.

The Hedge Fund Edge helps readers determine which markets and stocks are both lower-risk and higher-performing by providing crucial valuation and technical models, as well as crucial knowledge on stock selection approaches. Boucher also goes into great length about the relationship between macroeconomic performance and investment performance as well as the effect of governmental policies on the markets. A portfolio's allocation strategies with the aim of investing in the best trends across all asset classes at any given moment are also included, along with crucial timing models for identifying when to invest in gold, bonds, commodities, and other asset classes. The importance of diversification across asset classes, including distressed bonds, arbitrage funds, international hedge funds, various futures funds, and other market-uncorrelated investments, is emphasized in the book. Boucher illustrates how this diversity may be leveraged to create a portfolio that is impenetrable and extremely profitable, yielding huge gains on a consistent basis with substantially lower risk than the market. Boucher gives his own performance as evidence of what may be accomplished using these methods and offers instances from his own real-time hedge fund trading expertise.

The Hedge Fund Edge is a brand-new concept and strategy that combines market timing, vehicle selection, risk management strategies, economic insight and understanding, and tactical asset allocation to deliver spectacular returns with relatively little risk.
